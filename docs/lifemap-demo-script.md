# LifeMap Demo Script

## 30-second setup

LifeMap is an AI chief of staff for real life. Parents paste or forward the messy things that create mental load: school forms, doctor notes, travel details, bills, pet care, IDs, schedules, and reminders.

The promise is simple: brain dump in, organized next actions out.

## Live demo links

- App: https://lifemap-d33.pages.dev
- API health: https://lifemap-api.m-haslim.workers.dev/health

## Demo flow

1. Open LifeMap on iPhone.
2. Press **Login as Alex Kim** if the browser is fresh. If it opens directly to Today, explain that demo state persists in this browser.
3. Show **Today**: Daily Brief, Top Priorities, Your LifeMap, and bottom navigation.
4. Tap the center **Capture** button.
5. Paste this:

```text
Emma's field trip slip is due tomorrow, passport renewal is coming up before Maui, Milo needs vaccines before boarding, and I need the TSA PreCheck numbers, rewards card login, pet sitter details, and next week's school lunch calendar.
```

6. Press **Sort my mental load**.
7. Show the sorted items and explain that LifeMap turns messy context into concrete work.
8. Tap **Review items** or open **Review** from the bottom nav.
9. Point out that reminders and draft messages are approval-gated. Nothing sends automatically.
10. Open **Calendar** and **Vault** to show how life logistics become browseable.

## Close

LifeMap turns scattered real-life admin into a trusted operating system: what is due, what is missing, who we are waiting on, and the next few actions.

For the first presentation, demo mode is enough. Supabase account persistence is the next milestone once the production database target is confirmed.
